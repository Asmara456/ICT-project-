Job Search Guide for Beginners

Tips Resume Writing Interview Prep

Getting Started

Starting your job search can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be! Here are a few tips to begin:

  • Identify your skills and interests.
  • Research potential industries and roles.
  • Create a list of target companies.

Resume Writing

Your resume is your first impression with employers. Ensure it is:

  • Clear and concise (1-2 pages).
  • Free of grammatical errors.
  • Customized for each job application.

Interview Preparation

Interviews can be stressful, but preparation is key:

  • Research the company thoroughly.
  • Practice common interview questions.
  • Dress appropriately and arrive early.
